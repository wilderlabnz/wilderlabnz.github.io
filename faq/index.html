About us

We are an environmental DNA (eDNA) biomonitoring service based in Wellington, New Zealand. We specialize in biological water quality evaluation and the detection and monitoring of rare, cryptic and invasive species using new DNA sequencing technologies.

We can help with:

  • eDNA analysis
  • Monitoring of rare and cryptic species
  • Invasive species detection
  • Pathogen detection and quantitation
  • Water quality assessment
  • High-throughput sequencing
  • Bioinformatics
  • Custom R package development
  • Tagging and geospatial mapping
  • Web applications

wilderlab is owned and operated by Dr. Shaun Wilkinson, a molecular and computational biologist specializing in trace DNA analysis and machine-learning classification (see https://shaunpwilkinson.github.io/ for more information). Please get in touch via email or phone (64210588068) with your eDNA biomonitoring and biodetection requirements.
